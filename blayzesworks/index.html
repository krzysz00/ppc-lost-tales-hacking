The PPC:

The official PPC organization began with two girls named Jay Thorntree and Acacia Byrd. Jay and Acacia were the best and brightest assassins of the PPC, and also the founders. Our mission as The PPC- The Protectors of the Plot Continuum- is to hunt down Badfic and exterminate the cause of said Badfic. I work in the Department of Mary-Sues, and our general term is Assassin. We go into the Suefics, assemble a charge list, and exterminate the sue. Obviously, we aren't very popular with the Fanwriter population- and we shouldn't be. Our closest affiliate is the Official Fanfiction University of Middle-Earth, and its various spin-offs. Miss Cam is generally termed the authority in all things Tolkien, and she rocks. You will see some OFUM art on this site- maybe- and definitely some quotes, which I will have begged to use. OFUM is one of the greatest creations ever, and thus you must check it out.
