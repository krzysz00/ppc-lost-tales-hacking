LAMBDA'S LABORATORY
In days long gone, when she was still known under secular name of Anna and wasn't yet employed by the PPC, Agent Lambda was a registered pharmacist.  Her specialty used to be cardiovascular drugs, but being submerged into the craziness that is the PPC Headquarters, she has learned the importance of a variety of other pharmaceuticals.  She begged The Poison Ivy to allow her to use an adjacent room as a research laboratory.  Makes-Things graciously agreed to make some equipment for her, if only after having a cup of tea with The Poison Ivy.  Lambda promptly set to work on variety of drugs that would make PPC Agent's job a little easier.
